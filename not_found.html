404

HTTP error code (The Internet)

August 06, 1991

Who are you, and what do you do?

I’m an error message you receive when the page you’re after can’t be found online. You’ve probably seen me around and about. My job keeps me busy, that’s for sure.

What hardware are you using?

Some sort of rack-mounted device, I think. Maybe I’m virtualised? Or, I don’t know, a freaking Macbook Pro?

And what software?

An HTTP server. Also, TextMate 2. He he he he!

What would be your dream setup?

This is pretty much perfect, though I’d prefer it if people were more careful with what they typed into their web browsers, really. I could do with a holiday!
